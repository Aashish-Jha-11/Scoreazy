Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our program

How long does the mentorship program last?

+

Our program is flexible and can range from 3 months to 2 years, depending on your goals and needs. Most students see significant improvement within the first 6 months. You can extend or modify your program at any time.

What qualifications do your mentors have?

+

All our mentors are certified counselors or subject matter experts with at least 5 years of experience in academic guidance. They hold advanced degrees in their respective fields and undergo continuous training in mentorship techniques.

How often will I have sessions with my mentor?

+

Typically, students have one-on-one sessions twice a week, each lasting 60 minutes. Additionally, you'll have access to group sessions, workshops, and 24/7 chat support for any urgent questions or concerns.

Is the program available online or in-person?

+

We offer both online and in-person sessions to accommodate your preferences and schedule. Our online platform is interactive and provides the same quality of mentorship as in-person sessions. You can switch between formats as needed.
